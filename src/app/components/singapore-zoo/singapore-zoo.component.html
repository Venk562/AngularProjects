About the Zoo

The Singapore Zoo is a world-renowned wildlife park nestled in the heart of a lush rainforest. Spanning 28 hectares, the zoo is home to more than 2,800 animals from over 300 species including rare and endangered wildlife from Asia, Africa, and the Americas.

What makes Singapore Zoo special is its award-winning “open concept” where animals live in spacious, landscaped enclosures that closely mimic their natural habitats. Visitors can enjoy a truly immersive experience walking through elevated boardwalks, glass observatories, and free-ranging areas.

The zoo is also a leader in wildlife conservation and education, offering interactive exhibits, animal feedings, and keeper talks that raise awareness about the environment and species protection.

  • Enjoy interactive wildlife shows daily
  • Combine with River Wonders & Night Safari
